Why do we learn coding language ?

Problem solving

Coding teaches you to break down complex problems into smaller parts and approach them logically.

Creativity

Coding can be fun and creative, allowing you to use your imagination to make animated graphics, websites, and video games.

Career opportunities

Programmers and developers are in high demand, and coding can lead to a bright career with a high income.

Cognitive development

Coding can help children think differently and increase their thinking power.

Communication

Coding classes teach you how to explain code in writing and use it in a clear, concise manner.

Confidence

Coding can help children build confidence by giving them the opportunity to work on and solve big analytical problems.

Understanding technology

Coding can help you understand how technology works and how to use it in your daily life.

Working smarter and efficiently

Coding can help you work more efficiently, not just in technical roles but also in non-coding jobs.

Creating your own app or business

Coding can give you the ability to create your own app or open your own business.
